# Vision

## Purpose

Captures the product direction for Brain Loop.

## Vision

Brain Loop should feel like a local Codex-style mission control for project automation: queue-aware, thread-based, transparent, and safe.

Brain Loop should also be understandable as an opinionated open-source product: local-first by default, inspectable on disk, narrow in scope, and designed around Brain queue contracts rather than generic hosted automation.

## Product Principles

- Show what automation is doing.
- Preserve user control through approvals and pause controls.
- Keep every run traceable to queue items, logs, and Brain docs.
- Prefer explicit local state, queue transitions, and runner boundaries over hidden orchestration.
- Document unsupported areas clearly so the product stays reliable while it grows.
